Problem 1.2

The objective function minimizes cost. The total cost includes the investment cost, the operating cost, and the non-served energy cost. The investment cost is the sum of the individual generator installation cost times its capacity. The operation cost is the sum of the generation times the total time in the period times the operating cost. The non-served energy is the sum of the non-served energy production times the non-served energy cost.

\[ \min(COST) = \sum C_g^{INV}x_g + \sum\sum L_t C_g^{OP} y_{g,t} + \sum_t C_t^{NSE} nse_t \]

\[ y_{g,t} \leq x_g * CF_{g,t} \]

Need to serve load during each time period, so the sum of the production and non-served energy production should be less than or equal to the demand:

\[ \sum (y_{g,t} + nse_{t}) \leq demand_t \]

Installation capacity and energy production must be non-negative, which was established when the variables were created.
